Labii - Professional network for academics

Traditional social networks such as Facebook focus on personal life; industrial professional networks such as Linkedin focus on job searching and posting. However, people in academia seek to advance their research through networking. This not only includes tracking peers' research work, applying for and posting jobs, but also involves discussing research and seeking collaboration.
